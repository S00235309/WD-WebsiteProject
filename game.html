Hogwarts Legacy

This is a college project, completed as part of an assignment.

Introducing Hogwarts Legacy...

Hogwarts Legacy is an immersive, open-world action RPG set in the world first introduced in the Harry Potter books. For the first time, experience Hogwarts in the 1800s. Your character is a student who holds the key to an ancient secret that threatens to tear the wizarding world apart.

Game Features

Hogwarts Legacy is an open world game which means that players will be able to explore the entire wizarding world, including Hogwarts School and hogsmeade for example. Players will be able to choose their own paths and make their own decision weither that will be the main story or side tasks to up your level.

Magic Spells

The player will be able to have their dream come true and be able to use magic and learn their favourite spell or try and cast a curse.

Character Creation

The game's in-depth character creation tools allow you to rather accurately recreate yourself in-game as a Hogwarts student, or indeed make anyone you like thanks to some in-depth customisation tools.So yes, you can create your own character in Hogwarts Legacy and better yet, change their appearance however you like.
